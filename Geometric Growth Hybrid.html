Design Rationale

This hybrid concept merges the interconnected knowledge networks of the geometric approach with the clear progression visualization of ascending elements. The design maintains perfect minimalism while conveying both collaborative learning (network nodes) and measurable advancement (ascending bars). The gradient application from cyan to primary blue reinforces the journey from foundational to advanced knowledge, perfectly aligning with EnrichMed's mission to elevate primary care providers to specialist-level competency.
