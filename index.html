Design Concept & Meaning

Hybrid Design Philosophy

This logo combines structured data visualization with organic medical symbolism to represent EnrichMed's systematic yet human approach to pulmonary education. The design strategically balances technical precision with medical authenticity.

Segmented Structure

Four distinct circular segments represent EnrichMed's core educational modules: lung cancer screening, asthma management, COPD care, and pulmonary nodule evaluation. This modular approach suggests organized, systematic learning pathways.

Breathing Flow Foundation

Subtle layered ellipses create the foundational breathing pattern, establishing immediate pulmonary medical context. The overlapping horizontal and vertical flows represent the comprehensive nature of respiratory care.

Central Airway Symbol

Abstract branching structure at the center references bronchial airways without being literal. This element maintains medical specificity while remaining modern and scalable across all applications.

Color Psychology

Progressive blue gradient from light to deep communicates trust, expertise, and innovation. The four-color system allows for flexible application while maintaining cohesive brand recognition.

This hybrid logo positions EnrichMed uniquely in the medical education market by combining systematic precision with medical authenticity.

Four-segment structure communicates organized, module-based learning pathways that medical professionals expect from premium education platforms.

Medical Authenticity

Usage Guidelines

Clear Space

Minimum clear space equals the height of the symbol. Maintain proportions when scaling.

Minimum Sizes

Horizontal: 120px width minimum. Stacked: 80px width minimum. Symbol only: 24px minimum.

Typography

Primary font: Inter (weights 400-700). Logo text always uses Inter 600 weight.

Applications

Works across all digital and print applications. Symbol scales perfectly for app icons and favicons.
